About YourAIMate

YourAIMate, operated by Morning Glory Educational Charitable Society, is a platform dedicated to simplifying your financial and technological decisions. We offer three smart WhatsApp bots: CrediMate for credit card recommendations, AIMate for AI tool suggestions, and PolicyMate for insurance guidance. Our mission is to empower users with personalized, accessible, and friendly advice.
