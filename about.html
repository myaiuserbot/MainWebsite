About YourAIMate

YourAIMate, a brand under Morning Glory Educational Charitable Society, is dedicated to simplifying your financial and technological decisions through AI-powered WhatsApp bots.

Our bots include:

  • CrediMate: Personalized credit card recommendations for shopping, travel, and more.
  • AIMate: Discover the best AI tools for productivity, creativity, and business needs.
  • PolicyMate: Expert guidance on insurance policies to protect what matters most.

Our mission is to empower users with accessible, friendly, and smart solutions, delivered right to your WhatsApp!
