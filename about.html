About YourAIMate

YourAIMate, a brand under Morning Glory Educational Charitable Society, is dedicated to simplifying your financial and technological decisions through innovative AI-powered WhatsApp bots.

Our suite of bots includes:

  • CrediMate: Personalized credit card recommendations for shopping, travel, fuel, and more.
  • AIMate: Discover AI tools for productivity, creativity, or business, delivered via WhatsApp.
  • PolicyMate: Expert guidance on insurance policies to protect your family and assets.

Founded in 2025, our mission is to empower users with accessible, friendly, and smart solutions. We leverage cutting-edge AI to deliver personalized advice, making complex decisions simple.

Our team is committed to innovation and customer satisfaction, ensuring seamless and valuable interactions.
